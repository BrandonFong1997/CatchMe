About Catch Me

Catch Me uses an accelerometer, EMG sensor, and a heart rate sensor to detect when someone has fallen and automatically alerts a predetermined contact.

This device has the potential to save lives and enables elderly people to be mobile without worry.

Coded in Arduino C, this device makes use of the Intel Edison Microcontroller, and uses a script to automatically send a notification to certain contacts when a fall is detected. Once the user has fallen, the device monitors their vital signs to detect whether other complications have occured, such as shock.






The Impact

About one third of the elder population over the age of 65 falls each year, and the risk of falls increases proportionally with age. At 80 years, over half of seniors fall annually.
